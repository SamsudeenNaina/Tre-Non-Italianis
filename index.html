Welcome to Tre Non Italianis Ristorante!

We are Calgary's newest and hottest Italian restaurant. We are very different from every other Italian restaurants in the city because of our diversity! Our three founders come from very different cultural backgrounds!
When these friends decided to start Tre Non Italianis, they decided to each bring their special flavour and flare to their section of the menu!

Pasta
